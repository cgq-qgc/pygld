HeatPump class¶

class pygld.HeatPump[source]¶

Model and Data¶

HeatPump.hpdata¶

Performance data of the heatpump.

Heat carrier fluid properties¶

HeatPump.fluid¶

Heat carrier fluid type.

Get or set the type of heat carrier fluid used in the geothermal heat exchanger. The currently available fluid types are ‘water’, ‘ethyl_glycol’, and ‘prop_glycol’. The heat carrier fluid is assumed to be ‘water’ when fr is set to 0.

HeatPump.fr¶

Antifreeze volumetric fraction of the heat carrier fluid in m³/m³ (0 ≤ fr ≤ 1).

Get or set the antifreeze volumetric fraction of the heat carrier fluid. The value of fr must be between 0 and 1 and is assumed to be 0 when fluid is set to ‘water’.

Heatpump independent properties¶

HeatPump.TinHP¶

Temperature of the water entering the heatpump in °C.

HeatPump.Vf¶

Volumetric flowrate of the fluid through the heatpump in L/s

HeatPump.qbat¶

Part of the building thermal load applied to the heatpump in kW (- for cooling, + for heating).

Heatpump dependent properties¶

HeatPump.ToutHP¶

Temperature of the water leaving the heatpump in °C.

HeatPump.Tm¶

Mean temperature of the water circulating through the heatpump in °C

HeatPump.COP¶

Coefficient of performance of the heatpump.

HeatPump.CAP¶

Capacity of the heatpump in kW.
